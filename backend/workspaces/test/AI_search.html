Generative AI Search & Discovery

A Proposal for Media Prima Digital

Presented by: CloudMile

17th October 2025

Executive Summary

  • Transform Berita Harian's search into an intelligent, conversational "Answer Engine".
  • Leverage the power and scale of Google Cloud's Vertex AI Search.
  • Provide immediate, summarized answers to reader questions, increasing engagement.
  • Architecture designed from the ground up for cost-control on a high-traffic, public-facing site via intelligent caching and pre-generation.

The Challenge: Moving Beyond Search

  • Berita Harian's vast news archive is a valuable asset, but can be difficult for readers to explore effectively.
  • Standard keyword search is often limiting and fails to meet a user's true intent.
  • Readers now expect direct answers, not just a list of links to sift through.
  • The opportunity is to evolve from a simple search tool into a modern content discovery experience.

Proposed Solution: The "Berita AI" Engine

1. Data Foundation

Establish a robust data pipeline to sync news articles from the existing RDBMS into Google BigQuery, creating a centralized, AI-ready content repository.

2. Generative Answers

Use Vertex AI Search to understand natural language queries (BM & EN) and generate cohesive, summarized answers with direct citations to the source articles.

A Cost-Optimized Architecture

A multi-tiered strategy to deliver an AI experience that is financially viable for a high-traffic news site.

  • ✔Daily Pre-generation: Automatically generate and cache answers for trending news topics and FAQs.
  • ✔Suggested Questions UI: Guide users towards instant, zero-cost, pre-generated answers.
  • ✔Lemmatization for Caching: Normalize user queries to maximize cache hits, ensuring similar questions don't trigger new API calls.
